Honest fit

Who Throughline is for — and who it isn't

We do one thing: match career changers with coaches who specialize in AI-era pivots. That focus is the whole value, so we're upfront about who it serves.

This is for you if…

  • You have 10+ years of experience and too much built to throw it away.

Straight answers

The questions you're already asking

How are the coaches vetted?
We only list coaches who've personally navigated a major career change and have a track record guiding others through one. Before anyone joins the roster we verify their credentials (such as ICF certification), their industry background, and their outcomes. The whole point is that you're not gambling on a stranger.
How does matching work?
Tell us where you are and where you want to go. We surface the coaches whose own transition and expertise fit your situation, so you're choosing from a short, relevant list — not scrolling through hundreds of profiles hoping to get lucky.
What does it cost?
Browsing, getting matched, and your first intro call are all free. After that, each coach sets their own per-session or package pricing, shown upfront before you book. There's no subscription required to start.
What if my coach isn't the right fit?
The free intro call is designed to catch that before any money changes hands. And if your first paid session still isn't right, we'll rematch you with another coach or refund it — no awkwardness.
Aren't I too old to change careers?
No — you're too experienced to start over, which is different. People change careers in their 40s and 50s successfully every day. What stops most isn't age — it's not having a credible plan and trying to do it alone. A coach who's already made your move is exactly that plan.
How do I know AI won't just take the new job too?
Because our coaches don't guess. They match you to roles that are growing because of AI — where human judgment, relationships, and accountability are the product — not roles in line to be replaced. You'll understand the reasoning before you commit to a direction.
